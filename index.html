Wiski Fins: Carbon Fiber Surfboard Fins

Initially a gift for my younger brother, I optimized Surfboard fins for his size, weight, and existing surfboards. I then optimized hydrodynamic efficiency during turning through the use of CFD and Structural FEA analysis and manufactured the fins using the Forged Carbon Fiber manufacturing process. Eventually, my brother and I created Wiski Fins, and started selling fins to friends and local surf shops.

Mechanical Design, CFD Analysis, Structural Analysis, Composites Manufacturing, Mold Design

ZT Nano Heat Energy Lab

This lab in the MAE department at Cornell, led by the amazing Dr. Zhiting Tian, involves computational modeling and experimental characterization of energy transport. I worked with Phd Candidates Joyce Christiansen-Salameh and Gustavo Alvarez to research the properties of the 2-D Material Hexagonal Boron Nitride. I primarily assisted in data collection and analysis during experiments, wrote and optimized MATLAB scripts, and ultimately designed and manufactured a Dry Transfer Stage with PID thermal control.

Research, Mechanical Design, Electrical Design, Materials Science
